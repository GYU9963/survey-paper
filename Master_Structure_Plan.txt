PAPER OVERVIEW & MOTIVATION
================================================================================

This survey provides a comprehensive review of intelligent process control 
technologies in 3D Concrete Printing (3DCP), spanning from material extrusion 
to surface finishing. The paper systematically examines:

  - Hardware systems and robotic platforms for 3DCP
  - Process parameters affecting print quality
  - Sensing and monitoring technologies
  - AI-based diagnosis and path planning
  - Closed-loop feedback control systems
  - Automated surface finishing techniques

The key contribution is presenting an integrated view of how these technologies
work together to enable autonomous, high-quality 3D concrete printing.
